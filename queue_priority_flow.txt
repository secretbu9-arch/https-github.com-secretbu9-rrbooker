QUEUE PRIORITY SYSTEM FLOW
==========================

1. CUSTOMER BOOKS APPOINTMENT
   ↓
   [Default Priority: NORMAL]
   ↓

2. MANAGER REVIEWS QUEUE
   ↓
   [Queue Priority Manager Interface]
   ↓

3. PRIORITY ASSIGNMENT
   ↓
   ┌─────────────────────────────────────┐
   │  URGENT (Weight: 0) - Red Badge     │
   │  HIGH (Weight: 1) - Orange Badge    │
   │  NORMAL (Weight: 2) - Blue Badge    │
   │  LOW (Weight: 3) - Gray Badge       │
   └─────────────────────────────────────┘
   ↓

4. AUTOMATIC QUEUE REORDERING
   ↓
   ┌─────────────────────────────────────┐
   │  Position #1: URGENT Customer A     │
   │  Position #2: URGENT Customer B     │
   │  Position #3: HIGH Customer C       │
   │  Position #4: HIGH Customer D       │
   │  Position #5: NORMAL Customer E     │
   │  Position #6: NORMAL Customer F     │
   │  Position #7: LOW Customer G        │
   └─────────────────────────────────────┘
   ↓

5. CUSTOMER NOTIFICATIONS
   ↓
   [Push Notification: "Your queue position has changed"]
   [In-app Notification: "You are now #3 in queue"]
   ↓

6. REAL-TIME UPDATES
   ↓
   [Auto-refresh every 30 seconds]
   [Manual refresh available]
   [Live wait time calculations]

======================

1. MOVE UP/DOWN BUTTONS
   ↓
   [↑ Move Up] [↓ Move Down]
   ↓

2. POSITION DROPDOWN
   ↓
   [→ Move to Position #X]
   ↓

3. PRIORITY DROPDOWN
   ↓
   [Change Priority: Urgent/High/Normal/Low]
   ↓

4. AUTOMATIC REORDERING
   ↓
   [System recalculates all positions]
   [Updates wait times for everyone]
   [Sends notifications to affected customers]

PRIORITY RULES
==============

URGENT > HIGH > NORMAL > LOW

Within same priority: First-Come-First-Served

Example Queue Order:
1. URGENT - John (arrived 9:00 AM)
2. URGENT - Sarah (arrived 9:15 AM)  
3. HIGH - Mike (arrived 8:30 AM)
4. HIGH - Lisa (arrived 9:00 AM)
5. NORMAL - Tom (arrived 8:00 AM)
6. NORMAL - Anna (arrived 8:45 AM)
7. LOW - Bob (arrived 9:30 AM)

NOTIFICATION FLOW
=================

Priority Change → Queue Reorder → Wait Time Update → Customer Notification

1. Manager changes priority
2. System reorders queue
3. Wait times recalculated
4. Push notification sent
5. In-app notification shown
6. Customer sees new position
